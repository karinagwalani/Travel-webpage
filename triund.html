About the Trip

I put all my efforts to make your chapter of the trip, the most amazing one which you can read again and again in your picture gallery.

Detailed Itineray

    Day 0: Okay Tadaa Byee Delhi!
  • We will leave from Delhi around 6 pm in a luxury tempo traveler/Volvo Bus/Car.
  • Our first major stop will be at Murthal for dinner. (Own Expenses)
  • Antakshri, Damsharas, Dancing sessions and lots of interactive games throughout the journey.

    • Day 1: Mcleodganj
  • We will wish each other by “Subahh Hogyii Yaar! Ab To Sojao xD, Kitni Der Mein Pahuchenge “ instead of Good morning.
  • We will reach Mcleodganj in the morning.
  • Breakfast and some rest at hotel
  • Visit the very famous Namgyal Monastery, Bhagsu waterfall, bhagsunath temple
  • Reach hotel for an awesome dinner and bonfire night
  • How can we miss the dance party ? Get ready with your dance shoes and celebrate the trip.

    • Day 2: Triund
  • Wake up early and post breakfast get ready for Triund trek
  • Take a pit spot at Magic view cafe
  • Reach Triund by noon and enjoy beautiful views of Dhauladhar ranges
  • Check in to campsites and enjoy amazing star gazing, late-night games, Me-Me time to enjoy nature's beauty.

    • Day 3: Okayy Byee Byee
  • Post breakfast we will start our trek down.
  • Let's enjoy some more time in Mcleodganj
  • What about shopping or some amazing videos and photos? So much to do guys.
  • Post that we will also make a stop at the famous Dharamshala cricket stadium.
  • We will leave for Delhi in the evening and reach Delhi next day morning.
