B.U.T.(Budgeted Unplanned Trips)


    What is B.U.T.(Budgeted Unplanned Trips)?

  • BUT is Budgeted unplanned trips and not what you are thinking about. HAHA! This is an attempt to make people travel more and more at budgeted prices.
  • There is no itinerary, no plans nothing. The only thing which is decided before is the place and dates.
  • The average budget is decided beforehand and this money is to be kept by the travelers only. Wherever we pay for some food, transportation, accommodation, etc, the amount will get split.


    Who all can join?

  • Yes, there is a list of suitability guys. If you are more into nature than luxury these trips are for you.
  • If you are really fine with local busses, homestays. Join us.
  • We will be coming out of our comfort zones, making endless memories and learning new experiences. Yay!

    How are we making it budgeted?

  • By just limiting the luxuries. We will be traveling in local roadways playing antakshri all along. We will be asking for lifts to cover short distances.
  • We will be staying in homestays instead of any hotels or we may even put our own tents. We will be enjoying nature more than luxury.


    How to book?

  • You just have to pay 1000 bucks for registration. This amount is part of the average budget. You can redeem this amount during the trip. Cool enough?
  • There will be a telephonic interview before accepting the booking amount. If you clear that, you are good to go. Wohoooo!
  • Whatsapp group will be created a week before the trip where we can decide the leaving time and place. We all can exchange ideas of the trips to make it more happening.
