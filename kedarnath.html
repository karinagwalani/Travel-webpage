About the Trip

I believe that I am the Shakespeare of the travel and tourism industry. I put all my efforts to make your chapter of the trip, the most amazing one which you can read again and again in your picture gallery.

Detailed Itineray

    Day 0: Okay Tadaa Byee Delhi!
  • We will leave from Delhi around 10.30 pm in a luxury tempo traveler/Volvo Bus/Car for Rishikesh.
  • Refreshments like water bottles and eatables will be provided at the start of the journey.
  • Antakshri, Dumb Charades, Dancing sessions and lots of interactive games throughout the journey.

    • Day 1: Rishikesh
  • We will wish each other “Subahh Hogyii Yaar! Ab To Sojao xD, Kitni Der Mein Pahuchenge “ instead of Good morning.
  • We will reach Rishikesh at around 6 or 7 in the morning.
  • Breakfast and some rest at our campsite.
  • Local sightseeing in Rishikesh, visit Lakshman Jhulla, Ram Jhulla, Ganga Arti, and some cafes.
  • Reach the hotel for an awesome dinner and bonfire night
  • After dinner, the organizer will arrange several games like I never ever, Truth and dare, etc.

    • Day 2: Sonprayag
  • Have Complimentary Breakfast
  • Drive to the beautiful Sonprayag.
  • Check-in to hotel, click photos there and spend some time witnessing the eternal beauty.
  • Back to Hotel for a delicious dinner and fun games
  • Overnight stay in the hotel.

    • Day 3: Kedarnath
  • Wake up early and get set ready for the trek."Thakna mana hai"
  • Reach Kedarnath, check in to the hotel/camps.
  • Freshen up and proceed for the darshan. WOWW!
  • Dinner and overnight stay in hotel/camps

    • Day 4: Lasttt dayyy..
  • Wake up and have breakfast.
  • Enjoy the beautiful view.
  • Start your trek down.
  • Oernight journey to Delhi
  • Reach Delhi next morning.
