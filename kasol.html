About the Trip

Hi! I am Adventour. My focus is to write the most amazing chapter of your life by not just making your travel easy but also by making you celebrate your journey. I believe that I am the Shakespeare of the travel and tourism industry. I put all my efforts to make your chapter of the trip, the most amazing one which you can read again and again in your picture gallery.
I have 3 founders. The first one has done a Ph.D. in lame jokes and interactive fun games. Such a brilliant course! The second one is the human version of radio, he can speak and sing for 48 hours in a day! The third one is a box of encyclopedias, you can have an endless conversation on endless topics for endless hours with this amazing personality.
I have appointed these three to host your journey from happy to happiest. We are responsible citizens and want to make memories together.




Detailed Itineray

    Day 0: Okay Tadaa Byee Delhi!
  • We will leave from Delhi around 6 pm in a luxury tempo traveler/Volvo Bus/Car.
  • Refreshments like water bottles and eatables will be provided at the start of the journey.
  • Our first major stop will be at Murthal for dinner.
  • Antakshri, Damsharas, Dancing sessions and lots of interactive games throughout the journey.

    • Day 1: Reach Tosh
    We will wish each other by “Subahh Hogyii Yaar! Ab To Sojao xD, Kitni Der Mein Pahuchenge “ instead of Good morning.
  • We will reach Barshaini (Base for Tosh Trek) at around 12 noon.
  • Hike to Tosh ( 3.5 km beautiful nature walk)
  • Check-in to our hotel
  • Tea/Coffee and some complimentary snacks will be served.
  • SURPRISE! A dare will be assigned to each one of you and you guys are required to do that. :D
  • DJ night, bonfire and Dinner
  • After dinner the organizer will arrange several games like I never ever, Truth and dare, etc. and the best performer will get an amazing gift from the Book Of Trips.
  • How can we miss the dance party ? Get ready with your dance shoes and celebrate the trip.

    • Day 2: Kheergangaaaa
    Good morning Peeps! It’s a kheerganga day.
  • Have Breakfast and start trek down for Barshaini (the starting destination of the trek )
  • “Jai Mata di ka Jaikara” and let's begin our trek.
  • It’s a 13Km trek and “Kasam se kaafi sundar hai”. It will take around 4-6 hours all depending on your speed to complete it.
  • After reaching Kheerganga, It is mandatory to shout with joy before entering your camp :D
  • Tea/Coffee will be served as and when you reach to the campsite
  • Let's have partyyy now, with amazing party props and disco lights. "Karenge party saari raat" And yes, Dinner bhi millega :D

    • Day 3: Manikaran & Kasol
  • Open your eyes with beautiful mountains in front and relax your body in Hot Water spring
  • Have your complimentary Breakfast and start trek down to Barshaini
  • After reaching Barshaini, we will leave for Manikaran Sahib Gurudwara. You can have langar there, Kadi Chawal are seriously YUM!
  • After Manikaran we are back to Kasol and it’s shopping time :D
  • We will leave from Kasol around 8 pm and will be reaching Delhi by 11 in the morning.
  • As a part of our rituals, some amazing goodies will be provided by the team in the remembrance of the trip.
  • Okay, Guys! “Milte rehna saare, Kaafi meetups and reunions karenge”
